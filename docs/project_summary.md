# 📊 Project Summary — Revenue Leakage Analysis

## 🎯 Objective

To identify and analyze revenue leakage in a food delivery platform and provide actionable insights to improve pricing strategy and profitability.

---

## 🧠 Problem

Food delivery platforms rely heavily on discounts for growth.
However, excessive discounting can lead to **significant revenue loss**.

The key challenge:

* Identify where revenue is leaking
* Understand the drivers of leakage
* Recommend data-driven solutions

---

## 📈 Key Findings

### 🔴 Revenue Leakage

* Total Revenue: **29.85M**
* Revenue Leakage: **3.32M**
* Leakage Rate: **~10%**

---

### 🌍 City-Level Insights

* Highest leakage observed in:

  * Mumbai
  * Hyderabad
  * Chennai

👉 Indicates **inefficient pricing strategies in high-volume markets**

---

### 💰 Price Segment Analysis

* **Medium-priced orders contribute the highest leakage**

👉 Suggests **misaligned discounting in mid-tier pricing**

---

### 🏪 Restaurant-Level Insights

* A small group of restaurants contributes to most losses

👉 Follows **Pareto Principle (80/20 rule)**

---

## ⚙️ Methodology

### 1. Data Cleaning (SQL)

* Removed invalid values
* Handled missing data
* Standardized formats

---

### 2. Revenue Modeling

* Estimated expected revenue using reverse pricing logic
* Defined:

  * **Leakage = Expected Revenue – Actual Revenue**

---

### 3. Data Visualization (Power BI)

* Created DAX measures:

  * Total Revenue
  * Expected Revenue
  * Revenue Leakage
  * Leakage %
* Built interactive dashboard for insights

---

## 🎯 Recommendations

* Optimize discount strategy (especially mid-tier pricing)
* Monitor high-risk restaurants
* Implement city-level pricing controls
* Improve data validation systems

---

## 💡 Business Impact

* Identified ~10% revenue loss
* Provides clear direction for pricing optimization
* Enables better decision-making for profitability

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used

* SQL
* Power BI (DAX, Dashboarding)
* Excel / CSV

---

## 🚀 Conclusion

This project demonstrates how data analysis can uncover hidden revenue loss and support **strategic pricing decisions** in a competitive market.
